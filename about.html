About me

A bit about who I am
and what I enjoy.

Hey, I’m Aditya — a first-year Computer Science student at Purdue University. I grew up in India and moved to West Lafayette to study what I love: building software and untangling hard problems.

I’ve always been the person who’d rather understand how something works than just use it. That curiosity is what pulled me toward Computer Science, and it’s also what keeps me up late occasionally — chasing a bug, or learning a new framework for no particular reason beyond “this looks cool.”

When I step away from the keyboard, I’m usually playing guitar or digging into a puzzle someone swore was “impossible.” I like that both hobbies share the same rhythm as programming: small pieces, carefully arranged, turning into something that feels bigger than the sum of its parts.

Right now, I’m focused on soaking up as much as I can during my freshman year — classes, side projects, internships, conversations. If any of that resonates with you, I’d love to hear from you.
